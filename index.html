About Me

I am a Graphic Designer graduated with honors from Universidad Nacional de Colombia (Oct 2004). Former UX-UI Designer and Flash AS3 developer until 2013. I've been working as a front-end developer in Zemoga, with technologies like Node JS, React, Next, Angular, VueJS ever since.
Currently I'm studying Informatic Engineering at the Saint Thomas University (Saint Thomas Aquinas University) in Bogotá.
I love books and reading about -almost- everything: Sciences, Mathematics, Astronomy, Cosmology, Philosophy, Literature, Arts, History... I appreciate the Art in all its manifestations -except most of Contemporary Art, sorry-, enjoy playing piano (just some small pieces of Bach) and drawing with watercolours -botanical illustration mostly-.

Hello there!
I am Aiman, a graphic designer graduated with honors (2004) from the Universidad Nacional de Colombia, and currently I'm studying informatics engineering at the Universidad Santo Tomás.

Since 2013 I work full-time as a frontend developer using ReactJS, StyledComponents, Next JS, Express JS, and some in Node JS, Python and PHP (very basic with Wordpress).
I have experience in interactive design since 2004 (I worked a lot creating games and multimedia products in ActionScript 3) but I decided stop working in design after nine years in advertising agencies.
I occasionally do some UX/UI, branding, typographic, editorial and infographic design. I'll never stop design.

I loooooove books! I read about almost everything: sci-fi, literature, sciences, philosophy, art, history... Check out my Goodreads page. I'm passionate about mathematics, physics, astronomy, classical piano (still learning the same piece from Bach since 2010) and watercolour painting (botanical art). I'm new in writing about anything. I'm open to suggestions.
